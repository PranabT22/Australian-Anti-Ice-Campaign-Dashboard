About AAIC

Vision

Our vision is to cultivate a future where Australian youth and communities are empowered with knowledge and resilience, leading to a society free from the devastating impacts of ice. We envision a nation that recognizes the true nature of ice as a destructive and addictive poison, fostering environments that support prevention, recovery, and healthy choices.

Mission

Our mission is to educate all Australians—particularly our youth—about the dangers and devastating impact of ice, helping to prevent addiction before it begins. Through awareness, community engagement, and lived-experience education, we aim to put the freeze on ice, promoting the message: “Ice, not even once,” and building a healthier, safer future for all Australians. We are also committed to empowering individuals already struggling with addiction by guiding them toward positive pathways for long-term recovery and renewed hope.

About AAIC

Why AAIC was formed

The AAIC was formed to combat the devastating effects of ice addiction on Australian youth and communities. We aim to raise awareness, provide support, and guide individuals toward healthier, happier lives.
